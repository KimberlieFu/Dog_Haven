01

Can I trust Dogs Haven?

At Dogs Haven, we understand the difficulty of entrusting your beloved pet's well-being to someone else. We recognize that your pet is not just an animal but a cherished member of your family. This is why Dogs Haven takes our service very seriously.

Before working at Dogs Haven, all our carers and trainers go through a rigorous vetting process. Every booking is protected by Dogs Haven Privacy and Insurance Policy. In case of an emergency, you can rely on our FREE insurance benefit to safeguard your pet. Additionally, our Customer Experience Team is available seven days a week to assist you whenever needed if you have a query.

Furthermore, when you book with Dogs Haven, you can edit or cancel your booking or drop-by antime before the stay. This gives you the opportunity to ensure that your pet is safe and content before you commit to us.

02

Can I meet a carer or trainer before making a booking?

We do not have an official booking to arrange Meet & Greet. However, if you’ve found a staff you wish to know more to through Dogs Haven website, you can contact them directly. Or you can choose to use the chatbot to contact us, and we will communicate to the staff on your behalf. We are online 24/7 on the chat so feel free to reach out if you have any questions.

03

How is your service charged? How much to pay per hour or overnight?

Prices for different services can vary, and some services can charge per session or some can charge per day. Additional services may also incur extra charges. You can refer to our Services page for list of services and prices.

04

What if my dogs have special needs? Do you charge extra?

While making your booking, you can tell us what special needs your dog has. This can include food allergies, medication, living environment etc. We do not incur additional charge for any special needs. However, if your dog needs medication, please ensure each medication is clearly marked with your pet’s name and dosage level.
